math game

Math Pets is a learning game made for kids, and made by kids. Set the numbers or range of numbers you want to practice, then start the game. Every answer you get right gives you a coin that can be used to feed and entertain your in game pets. No in-app-purchases and no ads. The only way to keep your pets healthy and happy is to do more math! All artwork and game logic made by kids.
